Beginnings | How I started

"I first became interested in development in 2005 when I took a tour of a small startup college in Emeryville called Expressions. They specialized in video and audio development and every market related. My first thought was how I could use this for making my own video games. It wasn't until later in my life that I came back to this thought and began to make it a reality

Fast forward to 2015. I found some inspiration from those close to me and began to learn snippets of coding language and syntax on my own. I was getting pretty far with the basics but I knew I would need more help. I searched and compared and applied to different coding schools in the area, but ultimately chose to attend General Assembly' Web Development Immersive course. This course put me on the path of success and I never strayed from it!"
